Projects

Case study.

Project

Accounts Payable Intelligence Dashboard

Problem

Accounts payable oversight needed better visibility into payment timing, on-time performance, and discount capture across a large volume of vendor invoices.

Responsibilities
  • Analysed 5,000+ vendor invoices
  • Built and maintained the Power BI dashboard
  • Automated recurring reporting in Excel VBA
  • Documented data transformation & dashboard logic
Approach

Built Power BI dashboards to track Days Payable Outstanding (DPO), On-Time Payment Rate, and Discount Capture Rate; automated recurring report generation with Excel VBA; and applied Benford's Law for anomaly detection to flag irregularities in the invoice data.

Tools

Power BI, Excel VBA, Benford's Law (statistical anomaly detection)

Outcome

Strengthened financial controls and supported audit-readiness for accounts payable oversight, with end-to-end documentation of the data transformation and dashboard logic for governance and reproducibility.
